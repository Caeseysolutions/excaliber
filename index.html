GOT QUESTIONS?

Everything you need to know

Is the P-DISC NO3 really fully automatic?+

Yes. The P-DISC detects when your vehicle has stopped and automatically sets the arrival time. You don't press anything, open any app, or even think about it. Solar powered — no batteries ever. Buy once and forget it's there.

Does the CO-DRIVER NO2 require a subscription?+

No. The app, community data, and all warnings are completely free. You pay once for the device. No monthly fee, no annual renewal, no surprise charges.

Can I use the CO-DRIVER in France?+

Yes — under the same framework as Google Maps and Waze. French law allows danger zone warnings. OOONO complies and sells on Amazon.fr. Always verify current local regulations before use.

Can I use the CO-DRIVER in Germany?+

OOONO sells it on Amazon.de with a note that use while driving is the driver's responsibility under StVO §23. The P-DISC NO3 is fully E1-approved in Germany with no caveats.

Which product should I buy?+

If you park daily in blue disc zones — especially Germany — the P-DISC NO3 changes your routine immediately. If you drive regularly past speed cameras, the CO-DRIVER NO2 is your tool. Many drivers buy both.
